Tour Description in short:

 

Agra is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India, and for good reason. The city is home to some of the most iconic historical landmarks in the country, including the Taj Mahal. While there are many ways to get to Agra, the fastest train in India is the Gatimaan Express.

The Gatimaan Express is a high-speed train that can reach speeds of up to 160 km/h. The train makes the journey from Delhi to Agra in just under two hours, making it the fastest train in India. The train has been in operation since April 2016 and has quickly become a popular option for tourists and locals alike.

If you're planning a trip to Agra, be sure to take the Gatimaan Express. It's the fastest way to get there, and you'll be able to see some of the most amazing sights in India along the way.

Itinerary Details

At 6:00 am - The Pick-Up

A representative from our company will meet you there and assist you in boarding the train. At 8:00 am, the train departs. The Gatiman Express, India's quickest train, needs about 100 minutes to get to Agra. You will receive help boarding and settling in with all of the trip necessities. Our representative will provide you with information about the monuments, facts, history, and suggestions.

At 10:00 Am - The Taj Mahal

At 9:40 am when the train arrives in Agra, our vehicle will accompany you on your first adventure with the specially specified language guide. The Taj Mahal This sentimental reminder of love was created by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in honour of his cherished wife Mumtaz Mahal. The mausoleum is the world's seventh wonder and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is also a symbol of love.

At 12:00 Pm - The Agra Fort

You will go to the Agra Fort, an additional Indian cultural site made of red sandstone. After capturing his father till his death out of love for the city, Aurangzeb used this fort as his administrative base. The craftsmanship on the fort's walls and ceiling is the pinnacle of Mughal style and grace.

At 1: 30 Pm - Multi-Cuisine Lunch

As part of the complimentary tour, you will eat lunch at a restaurant serving a variety of cuisines. We'll help you get comfortable and will lead you through the city's many cuisines. You can also enjoy Indian fast cuisine like samosas, kachoris, and aloo chaat.

At 2:30 Pm - The Baby Taj

The Baby Taj, also known as Itimad Ud Daulah, is the final stop on the journey. The Mughal empire built this tomb in honour of a councilman. Before the Taj Mahal, one of the earliest monuments was constructed by the Mughal Queen Nurjahan as a memorial to the deceased and as an illustration of a heavenly afterlife.

4:00 P.M- Mehtab Bagh

Proceed to Mehtab Bagh, which is 5 km from Agra, with our local tour guide after lunch. It was created by the famous Mughal Emperor, often known as the "city of ghosts."

5:30 P.M- Agra Cantt

Transfer to the railroad station to board the Delhi-bound, rapid Gatimaan Express train.

7:10 P.M- Delhi

your arrival in Delhi, our chauffeur will pick you up at the New Delhi Railway Station. Transferring to the Hotel, Your Tour Comes to an End With Cherished Recollections of Your Same Day Agra Train Tour (Gatimaan Express).
